About EnerShift

EnerShift is a North West UK SaaS platform that helps businesses automatically shift energy use to times when renewable energy (especially wind) is abundant and cheap.

We make demand-side response simple. Our dashboard forecasts windy hours, recommends when to shift compressors, EV charging, or industrial processes, and helps you participate in flexibility markets like BiTraDER, DFS, and the Balancing Mechanism.

Why the North West?

We are building first in ENWL territory with HyNet decarbonisation projects and live BiTraDER trials. This gives us real local insight and fast feedback from early pilot sites.
