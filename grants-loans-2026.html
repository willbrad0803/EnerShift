2026 Warm Homes Plan Grants & Loans

The UK Government's £15 billion Warm Homes Plan (launched 2026) makes renewable energy upgrades accessible:

  • Low/zero-interest loans for solar panels, battery storage, heat pumps – available to most homeowners (repaid from energy savings).
  • Fully funded grants (up to £30,000 free) via Warm Homes: Local Grant for low-income households (typically income ≤£36,000 or on qualifying benefits) and homes with poor energy ratings (EPC D–G) in eligible areas – including many in Manchester, Liverpool, and North West postcodes.
  • Smart Export Guarantee (SEG) payments for excess energy exported to the grid.

Check full eligibility and apply details at: gov.uk/apply-warm-homes-local-grant or contact us for a free personalised assessment.
